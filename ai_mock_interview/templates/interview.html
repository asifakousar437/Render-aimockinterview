Interview Instructions

Please read these guidelines carefully before starting

Camera Requirements

  • • Keep your camera on throughout the interview
  • • Ensure good lighting and clear visibility
  • • Only one person should be visible
  • • Don't cover or hide your camera

Browser & Tab Rules

  • • Stay on this interview tab at all times
  • • No switching to other tabs or windows
  • • Don't open new browser windows
  • • Focus solely on the interview

Violation Policy

  • • 2 violations of any type will terminate the interview
  • • Multiple faces detected = violation (2 max)
  • • No face detected = violation (5 max)
  • • Tab switching = violation (2 max)
  • • First violation = warning, second = termination

Recording Guidelines

  • • Speak clearly and concisely
  • • Answer each question completely
  • • Use the recording controls properly
  • • Interview duration: 20 minutes

Technical Requirements

  • • Stable internet connection
  • • Modern browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari)
  • • Working microphone and camera
  • • Allow browser permissions when prompted
